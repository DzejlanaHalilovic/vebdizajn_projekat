About STEM education

STEM education brings together projects and resources aimed at young people provide opportunities for education and training in the fields of science, technology, engineering and mathematics. Here you will find information about programming schools for primary and secondary school students, online programming courses, robotics competitions, the annual Knowledge Olympiad and many other activities. We also publish on the portal teaching material available to all who like to learn and research independently.

Through a series of STEM exercises, we will try to bring the world of STEM closer as children so do their educators.

Along the way, we will try to stick to some basic concepts.

The first is that when working with children, we will use what is called exact mathematical concepts. During the exercises, we will emphasize the mathematical forms in the world that arms us, we will count the countable concepts every day, we will arrange the shapes by size.

Another aspect is that together with the children, we will pay special attention to the phenomena in the world around us. We will emphasize to children to pay attention in every activity to what they see, feel, taste or hear.

The third aspect is that we will ask children open-ended questions, to which we will not expect final and unambiguous answers. Through questions, they will master the essence of speech and become more relaxed in conversations with adults. children need to get used to something like that
